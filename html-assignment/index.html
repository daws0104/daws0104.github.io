About Me

I have an interest for many nerdy things. I’ve played video games since childhood and spend lots of time with them. Though this will only be my 2nd year playing I have put many days worth of hours into the MMO-RPG FF14Also I have recently gotten into TTRPG like dungeons and dragons.

My other big interest is art. I love to draw weather it be with a sketchbook or a tablet. My art is heavily influenced by the media I’ve experienced. For a long while I’ve been working on a personal project which is a multi-part comic series. Making my own characters and stories has been a fulfilling creative endeavor.
